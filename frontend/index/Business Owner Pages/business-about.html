About QuickServe

QuickServe is a food delivery platform based in Ruiru, Kenya, dedicated to providing fast and reliable delivery services from your favorite local restaurants.

Our platform allows customers to easily browse a variety of restaurants and their menus, place orders directly from their smartphones or computers, and have their meals delivered right to their doorstep. QuickServe is not just about convenience—it's about supporting local businesses by giving them a platform to reach a wider audience. With QuickServe, customers have access to everything from local favorites to unique, one-of-a-kind dining experiences.

For restaurant owners, QuickServe offers an easy-to-use platform for managing orders, adding menu items, and updating restaurant details. Whether you're a small family-owned restaurant or a large chain, QuickServe helps you streamline your operations and expand your customer base.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an all-in-one food ordering solution that is simple, efficient, and accessible to everyone. By connecting customers and businesses in real-time, we provide a platform that makes food delivery hassle-free, while helping businesses thrive in a competitive market. We aim to revolutionize the way people order food by making the process faster, easier, and more enjoyable.

Why Choose QuickServe?

  • Extensive Restaurant Options: With a wide range of local restaurants, QuickServe offers something for every taste and craving. From casual eateries to fine dining, you'll find it all in one place.
  • Fast and Secure Ordering: Ordering is easy, and payment is secure. QuickServe ensures your personal and payment information is safe and keeps the process smooth.
  • Supporting Local Businesses: By using QuickServe, you're helping support local businesses and giving them the opportunity to reach more customers.
  • Real-Time Updates: You’ll receive real-time updates on the status of your order, including delivery tracking, so you always know when to expect your food.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated customer service team is available to help with any questions or concerns, ensuring a positive experience every time you order.

How to Use Quick_Serve

Quick_Serve helps you order food easily from your favorite local businesses. Simply log in, select a restaurant, and place your order!

Steps to Order:

  • Log in to your account.
  • Select a restaurant from the list.
  • Browse the menu and add items to your cart.
  • Checkout and complete your order!

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
